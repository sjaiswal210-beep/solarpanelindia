About KalpDev Solar

Professional Engineers Team

KalpDev Solar is founded by Shubham Jaiswal and run by a team of professional engineers. Unlike dealers and resellers, we are qualified engineers who understand solar technology inside-out. Every installation is designed, supervised, and maintained by our expert team.

With offices in Kharadi & Wagholi, Pune, we have successfully completed 500+ installations across Pune and nearby areas. Our mission is to help every family and business switch to solar with zero hassle and maximum savings.
