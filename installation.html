Our Process

Installation Process

From first call to power-on, we make it seamless

1

Site Survey

Our engineer visits your location, checks roof condition, shadow analysis, and measures available area. Completely FREE.

2

System Design & Quotation

We design the optimal system layout and provide a detailed quotation with subsidy breakdown and savings estimate.

3

Documentation & Approvals

We handle all paperwork — MSEDCL application, PM Surya Ghar subsidy registration, and necessary permits.

Installation

Professional installation in 1-3 days by our trained team. Zero damage to your roof with premium mounting structures.
